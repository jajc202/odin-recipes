Salt and Chilli Tofu

Description

Chinese salt and pepper tofu is a great side dish when paired with some white rice and veggies. It's commonly served at Chinese restaurants and you probably have most of the ingredients in your pantry already!

Ingredients

  • 600 grams extra firm tofu, diced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp ginger, chopped
  • 1 red chilli, sliced
  • 2 stalk spring onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cane sugar
  • 1.5 tbsp Dry Sherry Wine
  • 1/3 cup avocado oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mix together your marinade ingredients.
  2. Pat dry your diced tofu. Add tofu to the marinade and mix. Allow this to sit for 5 minutes.
  3. To the pan, add in your battered tofu several pieces at a time, draining the excess batter back into the bowl. Fry your tofu until golden brown on each side.
  4. Finally, add in garlic, ginger, red chili, cane sugar, green onions, and dry sherry over your tofu. Quickly stir fry together. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
